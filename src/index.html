About the Author

A voice shaped by
travel, art & literature

Adam Nyang is a Gambian author whose work explores the intersections of culture, memory, and identity. Her writing has taken her across continents — from literary residencies in Ghana to celebrated circles in the United States.

  • Ghana Literary Residency, 2022
  • U.S. Changemakers Cohort — 1 of 18 writers selected
  • Scholarship awarded for novel development
  • International academic experience
